🔧 Configure CORS Proxy

Streams are blocked by your browser due to CORS restrictions. You need a small proxy to relay HLS streams. Deploy the included cloudflare-worker.js and paste your Worker URL below.

Quick setup (2 min):
1. Go to workers.cloudflare.com → Sign up free → Create Worker
2. Paste the contents of cloudflare-worker.js → Deploy
3. Copy your Worker URL (e.g. https://anistream-proxy.yourname.workers.dev)
4. Paste it below and save

Or use the existing public proxy (rate-limited):
https://corsproxy.io/? — works but may be slow
